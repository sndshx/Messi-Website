Who is Lionel Messi?

Full Name: Lionel Andrés Messi

Date of Birth: June 24, 1987

Nationality: Argentine

Born in Rosario, Argentina, Lionel Messi's journey began with a struggle against growth hormone deficiency. His talent was undeniable, leading him to Barcelona at age 13, where the club agreed to pay for his medical treatment on a contract signed on a napkin. He has since become widely regarded as the greatest footballer of all time, winning 8 Ballon d'Or awards, leading Argentina to World Cup victory in 2022, and winning 45+ major trophies throughout his career.
